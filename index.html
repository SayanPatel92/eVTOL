About eVTOL Innovation

At eVTOL Innovation, Inc., our mission is to transform the landscape of the unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) industry through the powerful trifecta of innovation, safety, and accessibility. Our commitment revolves around two core pillars: catering to our customers' needs and driving the commercial drone sector's safety standards to new heights. Our paramount objective is to democratize drone technology, making it not only affordable but indispensable for small to medium-sized enterprises. Notably, the inception of eVTOL INNOVATION, INC. is rooted in the groundbreaking scientific research conducted at the Unmanned System Innovation Center within the esteemed College of Engineering and Technology at Virginia State University.

Our distinctive expertise lies in pioneering the application of Electric Propeller Torque Arm technology to unlock the full potential of unmanned systems. Through our tireless pursuit of innovation in design and manufacturing, we craft state-of-the-art, energy-efficient hybrid unmanned helicopters and electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft with substantial payload capabilities.

Our eVTOL solutions are meticulously engineered to prioritize paramount factors such as safety, noise reduction, operational cost-efficiency, and elevated performance. We steadfastly believe that our technology stands as a cornerstone in the ongoing evolution of sustainable air transportation and plays a pivotal role in shaping the future landscape of urban mobility. At eVTOL Innovation, Inc., we are not merely pushing boundaries; we are defining the future of unmanned aviation.

One of our core offerings includes large payload drone cargo solutions. Our drones have impressive payload capacities ranging from 175 lbs. to 330 lbs. Additionally, we provide specialized agricultural spraying drones that leverage a mature agricultural aviation system. These unmanned helicopters excel in complex topographical environments, ensuring precise and flexible agricultural operations.

Our drone products boast a distinctive set of features, driven by our patented Electric Propeller Torque Arm Rotor technology, which offers superior efficiency when compared to multi-rotor alternatives

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

Optimized Air Movement

We prioritize efficiency by adopting the time-tested technology of traditional helicopter large rotors. This approach allows us to move a substantial volume of air at a controlled, slower pace, outperforming multi-rotor systems that rely on high-speed air displacement.

Torque Arm Advantage

Our patented engine power output, initially generated from the main shaft, is cleverly redirected to the propeller through a torque arm positioned three feet away from the main shaft. This design tweak significantly enhances overall driving efficiency.

Enhanced Safety Margins

A lower angle of attack empowers our drones with the ability to execute jump takeoffs and agile climb maneuvers. This not only adds a layer of safety but also expands the scope of what our drones can achieve.

Yaw Moment Elimination

We have eliminated yaw moments arising from lateral-coupling effects, ensuring precise and stable flight characteristics.

High-Altitude Performance

Our eVTOL drones are engineered to operate seamlessly at high altitudes and in high-temperature conditions, underscoring their reliability and adaptability.

Efficient Flight

Our drones excel at simultaneous forward acceleration and climb, delivering smooth and powerful performance that meets the demands of various applications.

Gyro Stabilization

To ensure secure autorotation landings, our drones come equipped with gyro stabilization technology, enhancing overall flight safety and control.
